# Security Policy

We take the security, integrity, and intellectual property of **Way To Sea** very seriously. If you have found a vulnerability or a copyright concern, please report it ASAP so we can quickly remediate the issue.

## Our Priority: Artist Integrity

The main goal of this project is to maintain the copyright of the original artists and ensure all visual assets are used with explicit permission. If you identify any asset that you believe violates these terms or is being used improperly, please follow the reporting guidelines below.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

For vulnerabilities that impact the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Way To Sea services, or for urgent intellectual property concerns, please send your disclosure via a GitHub Issue or contact the maintainers directly.

For non-security related platform bugs, follow the standard bug submission guidelines.

Include as much detail as possible to ensure reproducibility. At a minimum, disclosures should include:

- **Description:** A detailed explanation of the vulnerability or copyright concern.
- **Proof of Concept:** Steps to reproduce the issue.
- **Impact:** What is the potential risk?
- **Evidence:** Screenshots, links, or proof of original authorship.

## Submission Guidelines

- Do not engage in activities that might cause a denial of service (DoS) condition.
- Do not create significant strains on critical resources.
- Do not negatively impact users of the site or the reputations of the featured artists.
- Always respect the license terms of the individual assets found in the `assets/` directory.
