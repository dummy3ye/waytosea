# Contributing Guidelines

We’re stoked to see you’re interested in contributing to **Way To Sea**! Before you jump in, it’s important to understand that our core mission is protecting the integrity and copyright of the original artists.

## Our Core Principle: Artist Permission First

We do not host or display any visual assets that do not have the explicit permission of the original artist. If you’re submitting a theme, wallpaper, or asset, you MUST provide proof of permission.

### How to Contribute

1. **Asset Submission:** Ensure you have the original artist's consent to include their work in this project.
2. **Permission Proof:** Include a screenshot, link, or email confirmation of the artist's permission in your Pull Request.
3. **Credit Requirement:** Every contribution that includes an artist's work must also update the `docs/CREDITS.md` file.

### Updating `docs/CREDITS.md`

Your contribution is not complete until the artist is properly credited. Please ensure you add:

- **Artist Name:** The preferred name of the creator.
- **Link:** A direct link to the artist's portfolio, social media, or original post.
- **Asset Name:** The specific work being used.

## Contribution Standards

- **Aesthetic:** All contributions should align with the "Deep Sea" and minimalist aesthetic of the project.
- **No Emojis:** Per our guidelines, keep the code and documentation clean and professional—no emojis in the UI or assets.
- **Conventional Commits:** Use the standard `feat:`, `fix:`, or `style:` prefixes for all your commit messages.

## Code of Conduct

Please review our [Code of Conduct](docs/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) before contributing to ensure a respectful and safe environment for all artists and contributors.
